How it works

A consultative process from first conversation to delivered gifts. One partner, one point of contact.

  1. Discover

    We start with a conversation about your goals, recipients, brand standards, budget, and timeline. No catalog browsing required.

  2. Curate

    We put together package options tailored to your organization. You review, refine, and approve before anything goes into production.

  3. Produce

    Embroidery, screen printing, and full-color decoration happen in-house at our Michigan shop. Quality and brand execution stay under our control.

  4. Deliver

    Single-site delivery or ship-to-home for distributed teams and holiday programs. We coordinate logistics so you don't have to.

What we need from you

  • A sense of who receives the gifts and what occasion you're marking
  • Budget range and approximate quantity
  • Your logo and brand guidelines (if available)
  • Target delivery date

Don't have everything figured out? That's what the discovery call is for.

What you can expect

  • One dedicated point of contact throughout the program
  • Clear timelines before production begins
  • Samples when the project calls for them
  • Transparent communication. No chasing multiple vendors.
