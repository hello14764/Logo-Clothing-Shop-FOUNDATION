Oakland County Country Club — Golf Bag Embroidery

Direct embroidery on golf bags — rare capability in Michigan.

Client

Oakland County Country Club.

Goal

Personalized embroidery on golf bags for members.

Challenge

Most shops can only apply patches, not direct embroidery on bags.

Solution

Used specialized equipment (one of few in Michigan) to embroider directly onto bags without damage.

Result

Member loved the work and referred us to the club as a trusted resource.

Key takeaway

Specialized embroidery that reinforces prestige and exclusivity.
