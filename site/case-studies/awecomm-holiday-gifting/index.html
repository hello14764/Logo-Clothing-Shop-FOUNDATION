Awecomm AI Conference — Holiday Gifting Made Easy

Holiday gifting program launch at a Detroit-area tech conference — 20+ decision-maker conversations and immediate orders.

Client

Awecomm (event partnership) — technology and consulting professionals, ~150–200 attendees.

Goal

Introduce Holiday Gifting Made Easy — reposition from embroidery shop to brand experience partner that helps companies give gifts people are proud to present.

Challenge

  • Short preparation time to design, produce, and align all materials.
  • Stand out among technology and consulting professionals.
  • Intimate audience of existing clients and partners — trust and value, not hard sales.
  • New gifting offer needed a clear message that connected personally and professionally.

Solution

  • Custom microsite with curated holiday bundles, checkout, and follow-up automation.
  • Elegant booth (Rose Gold + Deep Blue) with stress-free gifting messaging.
  • Yeti cooler giveaway ($500 value) reinforcing product quality.
  • Conversation-first approach — helping attendees envision curated year-end gifts.

Result

  • 20+ meaningful conversations with decision-makers.
  • Immediate orders after the event and ongoing follow-ups with multiple companies.
  • Strengthened relationships with Awecomm leadership and clients.
  • Reinforced positioning as strategic gifting partner, not a commodity vendor.

Key takeaway

Branded experiences connect physical gifts with emotional impact — turning giveaways into thoughtful moments that strengthen business relationships.
