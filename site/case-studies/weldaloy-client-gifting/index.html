Weldaloy — Client Gifting at Scale

300 custom solar chargers to every client — then 500 premium cable kits shipped to France. They come back for quality.

Client

Weldaloy — aerospace parts manufacturer, metro Detroit. Norm F., Marketing Director.

Goal

Deliver high-quality branded gifts to their client base — gifts that reflect Weldaloy's standards and make recipients feel valued.

Challenge

  • Client gifts needed to feel premium, not promotional — custom presentation mattered as much as the product.
  • Scale: hundreds of units, each with consistent branding and packaging.
  • Follow-on program raised the bar — metal-housing chargers, decorated case, international delivery.

Solution

  • Program 1: 300 solar-powered battery chargers — custom box decorated inside and out, custom product placeholder, delivered to Weldaloy's clients.
  • Program 2 (~May 2026): 500 high-quality charger cables (iPhone + USB-C) in metal housing; unit and case both decorated; shipped to France for a partner/client.
  • Curated product selection focused on quality over commodity — Weldaloy chose us when they wanted it done right.

Result

  • Recipients loved the first program — Norm reached out in January: "Can we get any more? Great feedback — people loved the gifts."
  • Follow-on order in May: "I got great feedback from those. Do we have any more?" — 500 premium cable kits, shipped to France.
  • Weldaloy treats us as their quality partner for client gifting — not a catalog vendor.

In their words

“Can we get any more? Great feedback — people loved the gifts.”

Norm F., Marketing Director, Weldaloy

Key takeaway

When client gifting has to impress — and scale — curated products plus custom packaging and reliable delivery earn repeat programs.
