Averna — Precision DNA Embroidery

Fine-detail DNA helix embroidery when another vendor said "this is the best we can do."

Client

Averna — medical research company.

Goal

Embroidered North Face jackets reflecting the company's precision and premium image.

Challenge

Logo included extremely fine details, including a 1-inch DNA helix. A previous vendor failed to execute.

Solution

Adjusted stitch density, used thinner thread and specialized needles, ran multiple test samples, and fine-tuned machine tension.

Result

Client called the work "perfect." Even the highly particular CEO was satisfied. They continue ordering and have referred others.

Key takeaway

Precision embroidery that matches the highest client standards — even when others say it can't be done.
