Karmanos — Signed Boxing Gloves for Cancer Survivors

25 custom embroidered gloves — staff-signed and gifted to notable cancer survivors. Now on their fundraiser committee.

Client

Karmanos — nonprofit / cancer care. Program year: 2024.

Goal

Create a one-of-a-kind gift worthy of Karmanos's mission — originally envisioned for major donors, ultimately directed to cancer survivors the hospital identified as notable.

Challenge

Gloves with foam interiors made embroidery nearly impossible for most vendors. The gift also had to carry real emotional weight — not feel like promotional product.

Solution

Deconstructed the cuff, embroidered, and reassembled for a professional, durable finish. ~25 high-end gloves produced. Karmanos staff signed each glove personally.

Result

  • Gloves gifted to cancer survivors selected by the hospital — a deeply personal recognition moment.
  • Ongoing trust relationship: invited to join a 2025 fundraiser committee.
  • Continued introductions and connections — Karmanos treats us as a partner, not a vendor.

Key takeaway

Some gifts aren't about logo placement — they're about solving the impossible and helping an organization honor people who matter.

Note: Original brief referenced Tommy Hearns signing; final program used staff signatures for survivor recipients.
