Orfin & Associates — Gifting Catalog & Client Appreciation

80 blankets in 10 days when 4imprint couldn't — now a curated gifting catalog and custom holiday client gifts.

Client

Orfin & Associates — insurance and financial. Laura D., primary contact.

Goal

Reliable partner for monthly client appreciation and invite events — plus curated client gifting programs that are easy to reorder.

Challenge

  • First call: ~80 blankets needed with only 10 days until the event. 4imprint and big-box vendors couldn't deliver.
  • Ongoing need: monthly events, tight deadlines, personal service online vendors can't provide.
  • Holiday client gifts needed to feel premium — not generic promo.

Solution

  • Delivered 80 blankets on an impossible timeline — first proof of partnership.
  • Built a curated gifting collection catalog customized for Orfin — easy browse, easy reorder.
  • Holiday party client gift: fresh buttery shortbread drop cookies (baked days before delivery) in a fully customized box with lid and silver tie.
  • Ongoing: apparel, koozies, pens, ribbon-cutting scissors, event items — one point of contact.

Result

  • Laura D.: "You're my guy. You make it really easy for me. Unless you don't offer it, I'm not going anywhere else."
  • Gifting catalog live — orders placed as needed without starting from scratch each time.
  • Holiday shortbread gift was a hit with clients at their party.
  • Refers us to others when they hear of needs in other industries.

In their words

“You're my guy. You make it really easy for me. Unless you don't offer it, I'm not going anywhere else.”

Laura D., Orfin & Associates

Key takeaway

We turn "is this even possible?" into a long-term gifting partnership — deadline saves earn trust that catalogs never will.
