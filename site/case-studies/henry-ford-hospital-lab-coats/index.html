Henry Ford Hospital — Lab Coat Program

300–600 embroidered lab coats monthly — thousands delivered reliably over years.

Client

Henry Ford Hospital — one of Detroit's largest medical institutions.

Goal

Supply and embroider lab coats for hundreds of medical staff every month.

Challenge

Execution required a partner who could handle 300–600 lab coats monthly with strict consistency.

Solution

Managed sourcing, embroidery, and supply of all lab coats with professional finishes at scale.

Result

Delivered thousands of coats reliably for years, proving capability for massive recurring orders.

Key takeaway

Systems and reliability to manage hundreds of items every month with consistent branding.
