The Whitney — Hospitality Apparel Program

Ghost Bar vests, chef coats, and modern event apparel for a Detroit icon.

Client

The Whitney — fine dining / hospitality, Detroit.

Goal

Elevate staff presentation with a modern, cohesive look matching historic prestige.

Challenge

Previous vendors were transactional — generic products without understanding the restaurant's image or environment.

Solution

  • Ghost Bar sub-brand: logo concepts and premium vests.
  • Custom modern T-shirts for outdoor events.
  • White chef coats and black executive coats for leadership.
  • Embroidered hats and complementary pieces across departments.

Result

  • Staff proud to wear uniforms that matched the brand.
  • Guests noticed the elevated appearance.
  • Ongoing partnership for new apparel and events.

Key takeaway

Premium, purpose-built apparel programs honor tradition while keeping teams looking modern and cohesive.
